Timeline

Our Magical Timeline & Graduation

At this early stage, we focus on creating a nurturing and stimulating environment for your little ones. Through sensory play, music, and interactive activities, we help them develop fine motor skills, curiosity, and secure relationships with their peers and teachers. Each day is designed to foster joy, exploration, and a strong sense of trust.

  • 18 months - 2.5 years old

    Magic Munchkins

    In this phase, we focus on sensory exploration and fostering secure attachments. Our kids begin exploring their world with increasing independence and confidence.

  • 2.5 - 3.5 years old

    Magic Tots

    Toddlers begin exploring their world with increasing independence. We introduce structured play, simple problem-solving, and language development activities to build confidence and skills.

  • 3.5 - 4.5 years old

    Magic Explorers

    At this stage, we nurture creativity and collaboration. Children participate in group activities, art projects, and foundational learning, such as shapes, colors, and pre-writing skills.

  • 4.5 - 5 years old

    Magic Grads

    Preparing for the transition to kindergarten, we focus on early literacy, numeracy, and social-emotional readiness. Graduation is celebrated with a memorable ceremony!

Frequently Asked Questions About Our Daycare
